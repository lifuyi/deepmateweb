# Cities in China

Explore the vibrant cities of China where you can pursue your education and experience rich culture.

## Featured Cities

China is home to many world-class cities, each offering unique opportunities for international students:

- **Beijing** - The capital city with rich history and top universities
- **Shanghai** - China's financial hub and modern metropolis
- **Shenzhen** - The technology and innovation center
- **Wuhan** - A major educational hub with numerous universities
- **Harbin** - Known for its ice festival and strong academic institutions
