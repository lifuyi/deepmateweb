## Harbin Institute of Technology - Food and Entertainment Guide

## I. Recommended Restaurants

### 1. Huamei Western Restaurant (Central Avenue Location)

- **Address**: No. 112 Central Avenue
- **Specialty**: Century-old Russian western restaurant in Harbin, featuring classic Russian dishes such as cream-baked fish, pickled cucumber, and cream soup
- **Hours**: 11:00-22:00
- **Average Cost**: ~300 RMB
- **Why Recommended**: Representative of Harbin's western cuisine; experience authentic Russian atmosphere

### 2. Portman Western Restaurant (Nangang Location)

- **Address**: No. 12 West Dazhi Street, Nangang District
- **Specialty**: Authentic Russian western cuisine with live music performances 18:30-22:00, signature dishes include Moscow salad and braised beef
- **Hours**: 11:00-22:00
- **Average Cost**: ~150-200 RMB
- **Why Recommended**: Excellent atmosphere and quality food

### 3. Qin Fulou Restaurant

- **Address**: Nangang District
- **Specialty**: Traditional Harbin cuisine with northeastern Chinese flavors
- **Hours**: 11:00-23:00
- **Average Cost**: ~50-100 RMB
- **Why Recommended**: Popular local restaurant with authentic taste

### 4. Old Harbin Beer House

- **Address**: Central Avenue area
- **Specialty**: Beer and grilled seafood specialties
- **Hours**: 11:00-01:00
- **Average Cost**: ~100-150 RMB
- **Why Recommended**: Great for socializing and experiencing local beer culture

### 5. Dragon Restaurant

- **Address**: Nangang District
- **Specialty**: High-end Chinese cuisine with private dining rooms
- **Hours**: 11:00-22:00
- **Average Cost**: ~150-300 RMB
- **Why Recommended**: Excellent for special occasions and business meals

### 6. Home-style Northeast Restaurant

- **Address**: Near HIT campus
- **Specialty**: Traditional northeast Chinese home cooking
- **Hours**: 11:00-22:00
- **Average Cost**: ~80-120 RMB
- **Why Recommended**: Authentic local flavors at reasonable prices

### 7. Li's Small Tavern - Classic Northeast Cuisine

- **Address**: No. 413 West Dazhi Street
- **Specialty**: Classic northeast dishes with authentic taste
- **Hours**: 11:00-22:00
- **Average Cost**: ~57 RMB
- **Why Recommended**: Local recommendations, genuine flavors

### 8. Ulysse Pizza

- **Address**: No. 203 West Dazhi Street, Nangang District
- **Specialty**: Italian pizza baked fresh on site, clean and hygienic
- **Hours**: 11:00-00:00
- **Average Cost**: ~70-100 RMB
- **Why Recommended**: Quality Italian food in the student area

## II. Entertainment and Recreation

### 1. Central Avenue (中央大街)
- Historic street with shops, cafes, and restaurants
- Great for strolling and experiencing local culture
- Shopping and dining options available

### 2. Winter Activities (Seasonal)
- Ice sculpture festivals during winter months
- Outdoor ice skating rinks
- Winter sports and snow activities

### 3. Sun Island Park (太阳岛)
- Beautiful natural scenery
- Recreational facilities and outdoor activities
- Popular hiking destination

### 4. Movie Theaters and KTV
- Multiple entertainment venues near campus
- Karaoke rooms for social gatherings
- Movie theaters with latest releases

### 5. Sports Facilities
- Gyms and fitness centers
- Sports bars and billiard halls
- Basketball and badminton courts

## III. Practical Tips

### 1. Transportation

- HIT is located in central Harbin with convenient transportation
- Most restaurants and entertainment venues are within walking distance or a short bus ride
- Major attractions like Central Avenue and Gogol Street are accessible by subway

### 2. Best Time to Visit

- Spring, summer, and autumn are ideal for outdoor activities and dining
- Winter is cold but offers unique seasonal activities and hot beverages
- Avoid the coldest periods; dress warmly when going out

### 3. Budget Suggestions

- **Budget-friendly**: Campus cafeterias and home-style restaurants, ~10-50 RMB per person
- **Mid-range**: Specialty restaurants and branded cafes, ~50-150 RMB per person
- **High-end**: Renowned western restaurants and specialty hotel restaurants, 150+ RMB per person
