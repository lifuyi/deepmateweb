# Peking University

Peking University (PKU) is China's oldest and most prestigious comprehensive university, often regarded as the "Harvard of China."

## Overview

Founded in 1898, Peking University has played a pivotal role in China's modern history and continues to be a leading institution in education, research, and innovation.

## Rankings and Reputation

- **QS World University Rankings**: Top 20 globally
- **Times Higher Education**: Top 30 globally
- **China's #1 university** in most international rankings

## Academic Strengths

### Top Programs
- Humanities and Social Sciences
- Natural Sciences
- Medical Sciences
- Engineering
- Management and Economics

### Notable Schools
- School of Economics
- School of International Studies
- School of Law
- School of Medicine
- School of Engineering

## Campus

### Location
- **Main Campus**: Haidian District, Beijing
- **Historic Campus**: Traditional Chinese architecture and gardens
- **Modern Facilities**: State-of-the-art research centers

### Campus Life
- Over 200 student organizations
- International student community from 100+ countries
- Rich cultural and sports activities

## Admissions

### Requirements
- High school diploma or equivalent
- Strong academic record
- English proficiency (TOEFL/IELTS)
- Chinese language skills (for Chinese-taught programs)

### Application Timeline
- **Fall Intake**: Apply by March-April
- **Spring Intake**: Apply by October-November

### Scholarships
- Beijing Government Scholarship
- PKU Scholarship for International Students
- Chinese Government Scholarship

## Tuition and Living Costs

### Tuition Fees
- Undergraduate: ¥20,000-40,000 RMB/year
- Master's: ¥25,000-45,000 RMB/year
- PhD: ¥30,000-50,000 RMB/year

### Living Expenses
- Accommodation: ¥8,000-15,000 RMB/year
- Food: ¥15,000-20,000 RMB/year
- Other expenses: ¥10,000 RMB/year

## Notable Alumni

Peking University has produced numerous leaders in government, academia, and business, including former presidents, Nobel laureates, and industry pioneers.

3. **Strong Research** - Leading research facilities and funding
