# Fudan University

Fudan University is one of China's most prestigious and selective universities, located in Shanghai with strong programs in liberal arts, sciences, and medicine.

## Overview

Founded in 1905, Fudan University has established itself as a leading comprehensive university with particular strength in humanities, social sciences, and medicine.

## Rankings and Reputation

- **QS World University Rankings**: Top 50 globally
- **Times Higher Education**: Top 60 globally
- **Top 3 in China** for comprehensive universities

## Tuition and Living Costs

### Tuition Fees
- Undergraduate: ¥20,000-40,000 RMB/year
- Master's: ¥25,000-45,000 RMB/year

### Living Expenses
- Accommodation: ¥10,000-18,000 RMB/year
- Food: ¥18,000-25,000 RMB/year
- Other expenses: ¥12,000 RMB/year
