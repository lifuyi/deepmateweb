## South China University of Technology - International Student Handbook

### University Overview
South China University of Technology is a prestigious institution located in Guangzhou. The university is known for its strong academic programs, dedicated faculty, and comprehensive resources for international students. The campus features modern facilities and a welcoming environment for scholars from around the world.

### International Student Application Requirements
- **Undergraduate Application**: High school diploma, HSK Level 4 (180+ points) or equivalent Chinese proficiency
- **Graduate Application**: Bachelor's degree with relevant academic background, HSK Level 5 (180+ points) or equivalent Chinese proficiency
- **English-taught Programs**: TOEFL (80+) or IELTS (6.0+) scores required
- **Application Timeline**: Undergraduate programs typically open applications between September-November

### Recommended Programs
1. **Engineering and Technology**: Strong reputation with cutting-edge research facilities
2. **Natural Sciences**: Excellent faculty and comprehensive curriculum
3. **Business and Economics**: Modern programs with strong industry connections
4. **Social Sciences**: Diverse programs with international perspectives
5. **Humanities**: Rich academic traditions and scholarly excellence

### Career Prospects
Graduates have an employment rate of approximately 94%, with main career paths including:
- High-tech enterprises and technology companies
- Financial services and investment firms
- Government agencies and international organizations
- Multinational corporations and foreign enterprises
- Entrepreneurial ventures
- Further studies domestically and internationally

### Study Costs and Fees
- **Tuition**: Varies by program type, approximately RMB 8,000-18,000 per year
- **Accommodation**: Student dormitory approximately RMB 1,200-2,500 per year
- **Living Expenses**: Guangzhou area, approximately RMB 1,200-2,500 per month

### Scholarship Opportunities
- China Scholarship Council (CSC) Scholarships
- University Special Scholarships for International Students
- Government Regional and Provincial Scholarships
- Corporate-sponsored Scholarships

### Living Guide - Guangzhou
- Located in central China with convenient transportation connections
- Student-friendly environment with modern amenities
- Rich cultural heritage and contemporary attractions
- Diverse dining options and recreational facilities
- Easy access to public transportation including subways and buses
- Cost of living is reasonable and student-oriented
- Welcoming community atmosphere for international students
