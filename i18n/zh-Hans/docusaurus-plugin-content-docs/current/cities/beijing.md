# Beijing

Beijing, the capital of China, is one of the most popular destinations for international students seeking higher education in China.

## Overview

Beijing is a city where ancient history meets modern innovation. As the political, cultural, and educational center of China, it offers students unparalleled opportunities for academic growth and cultural immersion.

## Top Universities

- **Peking University** - One of China's most prestigious universities
- **Tsinghua University** - Renowned for engineering and technology
- **Beijing Normal University** - Leading institution in teacher education
- **Beijing Foreign Studies University** - Specializes in foreign languages and international studies

## Living in Beijing

### Cost of Living
- Accommodation: ¥3,000-8,000 RMB/month depending on location
- Food: ¥1,500-2,500 RMB/month
- Transportation: ¥200-400 RMB/month

### Climate
- Four distinct seasons with hot summers and cold winters
- Spring and autumn offer the most pleasant weather

### Transportation
- Extensive subway system covering the entire city
- Convenient bus network
- Easy access to international airports

## Cultural Attractions

- The Great Wall of China
- Forbidden City
- Temple of Heaven
- Summer Palace
- Traditional hutongs

## Why Study in Beijing?

1. **Academic Excellence** - Home to China's top-ranked universities
2. **Rich Culture** - Experience thousands of years of history
3. **International Community** - Large expat and student population
4. **Career Opportunities** - Hub for multinational companies
5. **Language Learning** - Best place to learn Mandarin Chinese
