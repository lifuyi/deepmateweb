# Tsinghua University

Tsinghua University is China's premier institution for engineering and technology, often compared to MIT in the United States.

## Overview

Established in 1911, Tsinghua University has grown into a world-class comprehensive research university with particular strength in science, engineering, and technology.

## Rankings and Reputation

- **QS World University Rankings**: Top 25 globally
- **Times Higher Education**: Top 20 globally
- **#1 in Asia** for engineering and technology

## Academic Strengths

### Top Programs
- Computer Science
- Electrical Engineering
- Mechanical Engineering
- Architecture
- Environmental Science
- Management

### Notable Schools
- School of Computer Science
- School of Architecture
- School of Economics and Management
- School of Environment
- Schwarzman College

## Campus

### Location
- **Main Campus**: Haidian District, Beijing
- **Former Royal Garden**: Beautiful historic campus
- **Modern Facilities**: Advanced laboratories and research centers

### Campus Life
- 300+ student clubs and organizations
- Strong entrepreneurial ecosystem
- Active international student community
- State-of-the-art sports facilities

## Admissions

### Requirements
- Excellent academic background
- Strong mathematics and science foundation
- English proficiency (TOEFL/IELTS)
- Interview (for some programs)

### Application Timeline
- **Fall Intake**: Apply by March-April
- **Spring Intake**: Apply by October-November

### Scholarships
- Tsinghua University Scholarship
- Chinese Government Scholarship
- Beijing Government Scholarship
- Schwarzman Scholars Program

## Tuition and Living Costs

### Tuition Fees
- Undergraduate: ¥25,000-45,000 RMB/year
- Master's: ¥30,000-50,000 RMB/year
- PhD: ¥35,000-55,000 RMB/year

### Living Expenses
- Accommodation: ¥8,000-15,000 RMB/year
- Food: ¥15,000-20,000 RMB/year
- Other expenses: ¥10,000 RMB/year
