# Shanghai Jiao Tong University

Shanghai Jiao Tong University (SJTU) is a prestigious comprehensive university with exceptional strength in engineering, medicine, and business.

## Overview

Founded in 1896, Shanghai Jiao Tong University is one of China's oldest and most respected universities, known for its rigorous academic standards and innovative research.

## Rankings and Reputation

- **QS World University Rankings**: Top 50 globally
- **Times Higher Education**: Top 60 globally
- **Top 5 in China** for engineering and medicine

## Admissions

### Requirements
- Strong academic background in sciences
- English proficiency (TOEFL/IELTS)
- Mathematics and physics foundation
- Interview (for engineering programs)

### Application Timeline
- **Fall Intake**: Apply by March-April
- **Spring Intake**: Apply by October-November

## Tuition and Living Costs

### Tuition Fees
- Undergraduate: ¥25,000-45,000 RMB/year
- Master's: ¥30,000-50,000 RMB/year
- PhD: ¥35,000-55,000 RMB/year

### Living Expenses
- Accommodation: ¥10,000-18,000 RMB/year
- Food: ¥18,000-25,000 RMB/year
- Other expenses: ¥12,000 RMB/year
